PyCon CZ 2018 Prague
1-3 June

Board Game Night

Board Game Night starts on Friday right after Lightning talks end in the Main room at Meetfactory.
No registration is required.

Rules of Board Game Night

  1. You do talk about Board Game Night.
  2. You do talk about Board Game Night.
  3. Anyone can play.
  4. More games at a time.
  5. Shirts all time, shoes optional.
  6. Games will go on as long as they have to or until 1 am.
  7. If this is your first night at Board Game Night, you HAVE to play. (Unless you don’t want to.)
  8. You can bring your own board games.
  9. PyCon CZ CoC applies to Board Game Night.

Also good to know

  • We will bring some games (DiXit, Pandemic, Steampunk Rally, Vast, Tzolk'in, Ubongo, Roll for the Galaxy, Robo Rally, 5 Minute Dungeon, Smallworld, Patchwork, Exploding Kittens, Hanabi, Carcassonne and more) but you’re welcome to bring your own as well!
  • We can safely store your games during the day.
  • While English or language-less games are preferred, there will probably be enough Czechs and Slovaks to play Czech games with.
  • Free pizza for every player!
  • Drinks will be for purchase at the bar.
  • If board games are not your thing, you are also welcome, just to chat or hack.
  • Musical instruments are also fine, as long as you are not disturbing others (you can play always outside or in another room).
  • Only for PyCon CZ attendees.
