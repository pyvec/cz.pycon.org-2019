Sponsor­ship

The PyCon CZ 2019 organizers are pleased to invite your sponsorship of the fifth annual, 3-day PyCon event in Ostrava, Czech Republic on 14–16 June 2018.
Sponsorship presents opportunities connect you with up to 450 expected attendees.

Sponsorship benefits

Sponsorship packages are starting from 20000 CZK and depending on the level they include:

  • Partner announced on PyCon CZ official social media channels
  • Logo on conference website
  • Logo on intermission slide deck
  • Roll-up at the venue
  • Booth at the venue or an option to get one for extra fee
  • Welcome package inserts / promotions for attendees
  • Promotional posts
  • Retweets/reshares on PyCon CZ official social media channels
  • Free tickets to the conference
  • Option to be an exclusive lanyard sponsor

Download detailed sponsorship information in our prospectus (PDF)

Custom sponsorship

In the case packages that work for many won’t fill your idea of visibility on PyCon CZ, you can become a diversity sponsor (provide free tickets for a target demographic), host or contribute prizes for a quiz or competition, become a sponsor of a connected event or even suggest your own idea.

Event summary

  • Friday 14 June to Sunday 16 June
  • Gorgeous industrial venue
  • Friendly environment
  • 300–450 attendees (professionals, students and hobbyist Pythonistas)
  • Volunteer community organized
  • 2 full conference days with 2 tracks (both tracks streamed online)
  • 1 full day of workshops and sprints
  • 30+ diverse, international speakers
  • Broad range of both technical and community topics
  • Social events
  • Food and beverages included in ticket price on all three conference days
