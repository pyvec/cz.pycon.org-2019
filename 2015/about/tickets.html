What's included in the ticket price

  • 2 days of conference
  • 2 tracks of talks
  • 14 talks
  • 2 delicious lunches
  • 4+ finest coffee with Czech cakes
  • ∞ pure awesomeness
  • all talks, sprints and workshops during the two days of the event
  • lunch and refreshments on both days
  • the finest coffee with awesome Czech cakes during breaks
  • a t-shirt and a bag full of conference swag and gifts

Accommodation and travel costs are not covered. However, we'll try to find you the best hotel deals in Brno.

What ticket is the right for me?

We try to keep the ticket prices as low as possible. Every corporate ticket allows us to issue two Student tickets. If you employer is paying for your ticket, please select a Corporate rate.

Do you want to buy more than 10 tickets for your employees? Contact us directly and also consider our sponsorship packages.

Where does all the money go?

This is a non-profit event run by volunteers and driven by the community. The event is organized with the help of Czech Python/Django community – Pyvec.

Everything we receive from your ticket purchases goes directly towards the costs of running the event and making it enjoyable and memorable for all attendees.
