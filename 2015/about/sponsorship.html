Become our partner

PyCon CZ is a two-day conference organized by the Python community for the Python community. The conference will include 1 full day of talks featuring 2 international keynote speakers (all sessions will be held in English), and 1 day of sprints and workshops where Pythonistas can get together and work on their open source projects.

This year is the first edition of PyCon CZ, and we are looking forward to bringing the tradition of diverse and insightful speakers, top-quality venues, and a great atmosphere to the Czech Republic. Regional PyCons are well-known for attracting developers, sysadmins, writers, designers, and community members from all over.

These packages are recommendations for your company or organization. If you would like to contribute a different amount or an alternative sponsorship format, please let us know and we’ll be happy to discuss custom packages.

Sponsorship Packages

Platinum €3,000 Sold out

As a Platinum sponsor, you will receive the following benefits:

  • Full width logo on the event website
  • 3 tickets
  • Display unlimited promotional (“Swag”) items
  • Your own booth in the venue hall
  • Address audience during a sponsorship lightning talk (5 minutes)
  • Space for copy in welcome/goodbye emails to attendees

Gold €1,500

As a Gold sponsor, you will receive the following benefits:

  • Large logo on the event website

  • Your own booth in the venue hall
  • Sponsorship branding, one of the following
    • Name the lunch
    • Name the pre-registration social
    • Name the main conference party
    • Lanyards sold out

Silver €750

As a Silver sponsor, you will receive the following benefits:

  • Small logo on the event website
