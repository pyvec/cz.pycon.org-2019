2020 2021 2022

The current pandemic situation has impacted everyone around the globe and PyCon CZ is not an exception. Unfortunately, these unprecedented times are hardly predictable so no one can say if we can expect favourable conditions to organize a big international conference with over 500 attendees in the foreseeable future.

A lot of events simply switched to the virtual world and keep going, but we believe that a conference with such a great community that we have around Python is at least as much about meeting people in person as about talks and workshops.

Therefore, it is with a heavy heart that we have decided to completely skip PyCon CZ 2022.

We are looking forward to meeting you all again.

Stay safe!
