2020

Due to the current situation and the special precautionary measures issued in connection with the COVID-19 pandemic, we have decided to cancel this year’s edition of PyCon CZ.

The state of emergency exists in the Czech Republic and we don’t know if it will last until June. We needed to make the decision now to avoid possible risks.

It’s currently impossible to predict any future developments for the rest of the year, and so there will be no alternative date for PyCon CZ 2020. Though it was not an easy decision, we believe it is a responsible approach. We don’t want to risk the health and safety of our volunteers and attendees, as well as the money of the Czech Python community in such uncertain times.

Thank you for your understanding and we hope to see you again in Ostrava in 2021.

In the meantime, you can follow us on Twitter and watch the talks from previous years on our YouTube channel.
