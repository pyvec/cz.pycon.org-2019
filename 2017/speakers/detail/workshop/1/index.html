Build (u)Python powered robot
a workshop by Věroš Kaplan

Let's build small lunch-box based robot powered by (u)Python. Bring you own mid-size lunch box, 9V battery and I will bring the rest – motors, wheels and a MicroPython board.

Please note, that you have to buy a separate ticket through ti.to. You get to keep the robot, of course.
