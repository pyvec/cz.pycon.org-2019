A Python and Rust love story
a talk by Armin Ronacher

Our experiences at Sentry of marrying Rust and Python and why it’s a great match for many applications. We will go over a quick intro to Rust, how to translate common concepts from Python to it, and how to make extension modules in Rust.

Armin Ronacher

mitsuhiko mitsuhiko

Armin is a passionate developer interested in web, graphics, realtime rendering and lots of other topics. He is one of the founding members of the Pocoo Team and a lead developer on several popular Python projects.

He is currently located in Austria and is working as a partner for Sentry and as an advisor for Fireteam Ltd. He is available for on-site teaching, presentations and certain types of short term contract work. Aside from that he does open source development and like giving talks at various conferences.
